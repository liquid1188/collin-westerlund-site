Work With Collin

Collin is taking on live dates, production work, and new projects out of his Baltimore recording room. Three ways in:

01 / GIGS

Book a Show

Live bookings — solo sets built on big vocal harmonies and old-school pop and soul craftsmanship. Listening rooms, private events, support slots, and one-off collaborations welcome.

02 / PRODUCTION

Make a Record

Writing, arranging, producing — and mixing and mastering — records for other artists, finished end to end in his Baltimore room. If you sing but don't have a band, a catalog, or a song to your name yet, that's exactly who he builds records for.

03 / PRESS

Get a Quote

Collin is open to comment on musical affairs — interviews, panels, and press inquiries on songwriting, production, home recording, and the craft of the old-school record.

Tell him what you're making, where it's happening, and when — he'll take it from there.
