Set Up In Baltimore

The recording room in the new Baltimore house is up and running. First order of business: new songs, tracked the same way as always — one part at a time, every instrument by hand, until the room sounds like a whole band.

If you're a Baltimore singer or player and you want to make something, the door's open. Here's how to reach me.

On The Record

What "Old School" Means To Me

People hear "retro" and think costume. I don't mean costume. I mean the way records used to get built — real harmony, real arrangement, a song that's trying to make you feel good instead of trying to be clever. Motown, Gamble & Huff, the stuff that still holds up fifty years on. That's the bar.
